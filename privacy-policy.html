Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Our Core Commitment

StudioLimb is built on a simple principle: your files and data never leave your device. Every tool on StudioLimb — image compressors, generators, formatters — processes data entirely within your browser using modern Web APIs. No data is sent to our servers.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Files you upload or process in any tool
  • CSS code you generate or copy
  • Images, SVGs, or any other assets you work with
  • Passwords, tokens, or any personal credentials
  • Personally identifiable information of any kind

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Some features use your browser's localStorage to improve your experience:

  • Favourites: The list of tools you have bookmarked is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key sl_favourites. This data never leaves your device.
  • PWA Preferences: Your decision to dismiss the install banner is stored under sl_pwa_dismissed.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for studiolimb.com.

4. Analytics

StudioLimb may use privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics (such as aggregated page view counts) to understand which tools are most popular. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Advertising

StudioLimb displays advertisements via Google AdSense to support free access to all tools. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

6. Third-Party Services

The following third-party resources are loaded by this site:

  • Google Fonts — typography (font files loaded from fonts.googleapis.com)
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — UI framework
  • Google AdSense — advertising

These services have their own privacy policies and may set cookies or log access data according to their terms.

7. Cookies

StudioLimb itself does not set any cookies. Google AdSense may set advertising cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

StudioLimb does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The tools are designed for web designers and developers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of StudioLimb after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
