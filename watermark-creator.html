About Watermark Creator

Add text or logo watermarks to images directly in your browser. Upload a photo, configure watermark text or upload a PNG logo, adjust opacity, position, size, and rotation, then download the watermarked image. Useful for photographers, designers, and anyone protecting visual work from casual reposting.

How It Works

  1. Upload your photo (drag-and-drop or click to browse)
  2. Choose text watermark or upload a logo (PNG with transparency recommended)
  3. Adjust opacity (30-50% subtle, 60-80% protective), position, size
  4. Download the watermarked image as JPG or PNG

Common Use Cases

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the watermark reduce image quality?
No — the watermark is composited onto the image at high quality. As long as you save at JPG 85%+ or PNG, the underlying image quality is unchanged.
Can AI tools remove watermarks?
Modern inpainting AI (Photoshop Generative Fill, specialized tools) can remove simple corner watermarks. Watermarks crossing the subject are harder to remove cleanly. Combine watermark + EXIF data + copyright registration for stronger protection.
What's the best watermark opacity?
30-50% is the standard sweet spot — visible but not ruining the image. 10-20% is subtle branding; 60-80% is intentional protection. 100% should be reserved for "DO NOT TAKE" preview-only images.
